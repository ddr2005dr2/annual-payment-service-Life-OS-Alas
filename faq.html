Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about how LifeOS Atlas works and how it can be used.

Do I have to use LifeOS Atlas for everything?

No. You can use it for one focused area such as reminders, documents, appointments, household coordination, or follow-up tracking, then expand later only if it helps.

What kinds of things can I manage here?

Examples include MVA renewal reminders, doctor appointments, household schedules, important document tracking, personal admin, and recurring life responsibilities.

How do alerts work?

The system records reminder-style events and is structured for email notifications and deadline visibility.

Is it paid right now?

Access is currently open. If billing is activated later, advance notice will be provided before charges begin.
