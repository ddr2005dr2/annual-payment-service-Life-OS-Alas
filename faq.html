FAQ

Answers to common questions about how LifeOS Atlas can be used and how access works.

What is LifeOS Atlas?

It is a private life operations platform that helps adults organize tasks, reminders, documents, planning, support, and other day-to-day responsibilities in one place.

Do I have to use it for everything?

No. You can start with one use case, such as document organization, reminders, or task tracking, and expand only if it helps.

Who is it for?

It is for people who want a more organized and less fragmented way to manage the moving parts of adult life.

Is it free right now?

Access is currently open. If billing is activated later, advance notice will be provided before charges begin.
