What does LifeOS Atlas actually help with?

It helps users organize reminders, obligations, deadlines, bills, routines, and guided next steps in one product layer so fewer important things slip through the cracks.

Is the concierge a human?

No. The concierge is an AI-guided product concierge designed to help with LifeOS Atlas questions, onboarding, workflows, and support topics only.

Will subscriptions be active immediately?

No. Entry can remain free while the business evaluates traction. Other lawful monetization channels may operate before recurring subscriptions are switched on.

How is data protected?

The platform is intended to use strong encryption, strict access control, minimal-permission integrations, and transparent privacy and deletion controls.

Can I cancel later?

Yes. Users should be able to cancel future paid plans before renewal. Cancellation stops future recurring billing, but it does not create a refund for digital services already delivered.

Do you offer refunds?

For digital services, the policy can state that fulfilled digital access, configuration, or delivered service is generally non-refundable when clearly disclosed before purchase.
