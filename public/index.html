Security and trust

The platform should communicate visible trust architecture across the front end, middle layer, and backend so users understand the product is secure and deliberate.

Encryption

Design target: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 at rest, encrypted backups, and KMS-managed key controls.

Access control

Least-privilege permissions, MFA-capable authentication, server-side secret handling, and audited administrative actions.

User safety

Minimal permission requests, plain-language disclosures, visible policy pages, and deletion and cancellation pathways that are easy to understand.
