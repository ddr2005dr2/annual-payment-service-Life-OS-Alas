# LifeOS Atlas policy starter pack

## Privacy Policy
LifeOS Atlas should explain what information is collected, why it is collected, how it is protected, how long it is retained, and how users can request deletion where applicable.

## Terms of Service
The terms should define use of the service, user responsibilities, platform limitations, and rules around acceptable conduct and automation boundaries.

## Refund Policy
Digital services, fulfilled access, configured setups, and delivered software service value are generally non-refundable once delivered, unless required otherwise by law.

## Cancellation Policy
Users may cancel future renewals of paid plans before the next billing cycle. Cancellation stops future recurring charges but does not create a refund for already delivered digital service.

## AI Disclosure
The concierge is an AI-powered product guide limited to LifeOS Atlas support, onboarding, billing explanation, workflow guidance, and related company knowledge. It does not impersonate a human.

## Data Deletion
Users should be able to request account deletion and associated data deletion according to applicable law, platform rules, and retention obligations.
